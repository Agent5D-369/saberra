What's inside the Living Memory Hub

Seven databases. One organizational memory.

The Notion template includes the full structure Saberra deploys — all seven record types, sample records you can inspect, and the review queue where Sera proposes and humans approve.

Decisions

Every significant organizational decision captured with context, rationale, owner, and the meeting or email where it was made.

Tasks & Commitments

Action items extracted from meetings with owner, due date, and the source record that created them. Nothing falls through because someone left.

Risks & Flags

Concerns, dependencies, and blockers surfaced from meetings and email. Reviewed and tracked, not buried in a transcript.

Roles & Responsibilities

Who owns what, when the role was assigned, and what changed during transitions. Institutional role history that survives turnover.

Policies & Agreements

Organizational rules, vendor agreements, and standing decisions. Searchable and traceable back to when and why they were created.

Meetings

Source meeting records linking to every decision, task, risk, and role change extracted from that session.

Sources

Every email thread, document, or external record that informed a reviewed decision or commitment. Full traceability.
