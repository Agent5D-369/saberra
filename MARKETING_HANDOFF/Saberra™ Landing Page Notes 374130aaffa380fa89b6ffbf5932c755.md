# Saberra™ Landing Page Notes.

What to keep from this brief:

- "Ask your organization what it already knows." - this is the headline. Don't debate it further.
- Segment 1 (Notion-native, 20-200 people) as primary - correct.
- The zero behavior change angle is the most powerful differentiator and it's underemphasized in this brief. It belongs in the hero.
- "We don't need more notes. We need memory." - the sharpest competitive line against Fireflies/Otter/Fathom.

What to sharpen:
The brief lists 12 pain categories. A landing page can hold three. The three that convert best together are: key person risk ("if she leaves we're screwed"), decision loss ("didn't we already decide this?"), and zero behavior change ("please don't give us another tool"). Those three hit the buyer emotionally, intellectually, and practically in sequence.
