# Living Memory Hub - One Pager

## The Problem

Every organization bleeds knowledge. Decisions made in meetings are forgotten. Tasks assigned in email threads disappear. When a key person leaves, they take years of context with them. New hires take six months to become productive because nothing is written down. Researchers estimate organizations waste 20-35% of annual payroll on duplicated work, repeated decisions, and errors made in the absence of context.

The knowledge is being created. It lives in your meeting recordings, your transcripts, your email threads. Nobody captures it. Nobody can search it. Nobody can ask it a question.

## The Solution

**Living Memory Hub** is an always-on AI system that automatically captures everything that happens in your organization and turns it into a searchable, structured institutional memory.

**Sera**, the AI at the center of the system, remembers every meeting, every decision, every task, every risk, every policy discussion - and answers questions about your organization's history in plain language, in seconds.

## How It Works

1. Your team keeps using Google Meet exactly as they do now
2. When a meeting ends, Google sends the transcript and AI notes to a monitored inbox
3. Living Memory Hub reads those emails, processes the content with Claude AI, and extracts: participants, decisions, tasks, risks, projects, roles, policies, and more
4. Everything goes into 17 structured Notion databases for human review
5. Nothing becomes permanent memory without human approval
6. Sera can answer any question about the organization's history, sourced from that memory

**Zero behavior change for your team. Zero new tools to log into. Zero meetings to rethink.**
