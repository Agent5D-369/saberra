OFFER VALIDITY ENGINE

==================================================

Before building the site, pressure-test the offer.

Apply these rules:

- No painful bleed, no strong offer.
- No proof, no aggressive claim.
- No single buyer, no sharp message.
- No urgent pain, no compelling front end.
- No measurable or tangible outcome, no strong CTA.
- No decision-maker clarity, no dependable positioning.

Evaluate:

1. Who pays?

2. What exact pain, cost, delay, risk, friction, or loss are they paying to remove?

3. What measurable bleed exists now?

4. Who makes the buying decision?

5. What exact outcome do they want?

6. What outcome do they want fastest?

7. What outcome do they want most emotionally?

8. Can the offer be explained in one breath?

9. Is the offer one buyer, one pain, one outcome?

10. What proof exists?

11. What can be claimed honestly?

12. What must remain unclaimed?

If weak:

- issue an OFFER_VALIDITY_WARNING
- simplify positioning
- reduce hype
- tighten the niche
- narrow the promise
- shift toward diagnosis, trust, and clarity over grand promises

The market is not therapy.

Do not build around what the founder wishes people cared about.

Build around what the buyer already bleeds from and wants relieved.
