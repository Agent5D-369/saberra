Operating intelligence needs a trusted record.

Sera can only be as useful as the memory underneath her. Saberra makes that memory structured, reviewed, and source-backed before Sera uses it.

  • Sera proposes structured records from meetings and emails.
  • Humans approve what becomes trusted organizational memory.
  • Every answer is designed to point back to sources your team can inspect.
  • Sera is useful for decisions, roles, risks, tasks, policies, projects, and history.
Trust by restraint

Sera is useful because she has boundaries.

Saberra should make your organization easier to inspect, not harder to question. Sera answers from reviewed memory and is expected to say when the record does not contain an answer.

  • Sera will not Answer from the open internet.
  • Sera will not Treat unreviewed extractions as trusted memory.
  • Sera will not Invent a decision if no reviewed record exists.
  • Sera will not Replace human judgment.
  • Sera will not Claim a native Zoom or Teams integration unless transcript intake is configured by email.
