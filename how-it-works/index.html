A dedicated inbox receives Google Meet outputs and important operational email. AI extraction proposes structured records. A human reviews the queue. Approved records become the source Sera can answer from.

  • Google Meet recordings, transcripts, and Gemini notes are captured through email.

  • Records enter 20 structured Notion databases with status and source traceability.
