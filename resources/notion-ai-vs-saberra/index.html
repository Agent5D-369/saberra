Notion AI answers from what exists. Saberra helps create the record.

Notion AI is valuable when the right context is already written down. Saberra solves the upstream problem: meetings, emailed transcripts, email threads, decisions, risks, and roles that never become structured memory.

The gap is not search. The gap is capture and review.

Many teams already have Notion. The issue is that people do not consistently convert meetings and email into durable records. Saberra uses Notion as the default memory backend, then adds capture, extraction, human review, and Sera retrieval.

  • Notion AI helps with content already in Notion.
  • Saberra captures meeting and email output before it disappears.
  • Human review keeps trusted memory inspectable.
  • Sera answers from reviewed records with source context.

Your Notion workspace can become the record your team actually trusts.
