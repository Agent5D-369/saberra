Common questions before a setup call.

Is our data private?

Yes. Records live in your Google Workspace, Notion workspace, and configured accounts. Your team keeps control.

How much setup is required?

The standard deployment is four guided weeks: source routing, hub configuration, review workflow, and operating baseline.

What if we already use Notion?

That is usually an advantage. Saberra keeps the memory hub current instead of asking humans to update it after every meeting.

No. Meeting summaries are raw material. Saberra turns them into reviewed decisions, tasks, risks, roles, and source-backed answers.
